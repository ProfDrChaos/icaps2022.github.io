ICAPS Code of Conduct

It is against the ICAPS Code of Conduct to engage in discriminatory harassment against individuals participating at ICAPS. If you have been harassed or disparaged in violation of the ICAPS Code of Conduct, please report the incident through the ICAPS Incident Report Form.
